          HOW TO DOWNLOAD

          Option 1: Download our SK Launcher (Recommended)
          • Install the launcher on your device
          • In Multiplayer, click Add Server.
          • Enter our server IP: game.retrocraft.io

          Save and join the fun!

          Option 2: Use the Official Minecraft Launcher
          • Go to the official Minecraft website: minecraft.net/download
          • Download and install the Minecraft Launcher for your device.
          • Log in with your Minecraft account.
          • In Multiplayer, click Add Server.
          • Enter our server IP: game.retrocraft.io

          Save and join the fun!
